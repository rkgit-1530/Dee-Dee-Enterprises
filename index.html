About Us

With over four decades of experience, Dee Dee Enterprises stands as a reliable name in civil works, land approvals, and building consultations. We pride ourselves on delivering quality, trust, and timely service.

  • Over 40 Years of Experience in the industry.
  • Deep understanding of local building regulations and approvals
  • Client-centric approach with personal guidance throughout the process

Founded by Mr. Ravikumar N, Dee Dee Enterprises has been serving Chennai since 1980. We specialize in civil construction services, building approvals, and land-related legal assistance. Our goal is to simplify your property and construction needs with professionalism and integrity.
